About Us

Serving Raniganj Since 2002

Bajrang General Store has been a trusted name in Raniganj, Jagdishpur, Amethi for over two decades. We specialize in quality clothing for the entire family.

From everyday wear to special occasions, we offer a wide range of products at affordable prices with friendly service.

Our Promise: Quality fabrics, fair prices, and treating every customer like family.

Meet Our Owner

Sanjay Tiwari

Farmer & Entrepreneur

Sanjay Tiwari is not just a shopkeeper ‚Äì he's a dedicated farmer and community leader who embodies the spirit of hard work and service.

Daily Routine: Sanjay wakes up at 5:00 AM every morning to work on his farm, tending to crops and livestock. After completing his farming duties, he opens Bajrang General Store at 10:00 AM to serve the community.

Community Leadership: Beyond running the store, Sanjay is actively involved in village development initiatives, helps poor people, and provides employment opportunities through farming. His commitment to the community has made him a respected figure in Raniganj.

With over 20 years of experience in the clothing business, Sanjay personally selects quality products that meet the needs of local families.
